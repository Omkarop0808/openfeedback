Our Mission

"Share Your Voice, Shape the Future"

OpenFeedback was built with a simple idea: everyone deserves a platform to share honest opinions about the products and services they use every day. We believe that transparent, community-driven feedback helps companies improve and empowers consumers to make informed decisions. Our goal is to bridge the gap between users and companies by creating a space where authentic voices are heard, valued, and acted upon.

What We Offer

OpenFeedback is packed with features designed to make the feedback experience seamless and meaningful.

AI Sentiment Analysis

Every piece of feedback is automatically analyzed using the VADER machine learning model to detect positive, negative, or neutral sentiment.

Moderated Content

All submissions go through an admin moderation queue to ensure quality and prevent abuse before appearing publicly.

Community Voting

Upvote or downvote feedback to surface the most helpful and relevant opinions from the community.

Search & Filter

Easily find feedback by company, sentiment, or keywords with powerful search and filtering capabilities.

Secure Authentication

User accounts with secure password hashing, session management, and role-based access control for admins.

Responsive Design

A beautiful, modern interface that works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Sign up with a username, email, and password to join the community.

2

Choose a Company

Select from our list of supported companies or enter a custom company name.

3

Share Your Feedback

Write your honest opinion. Our AI automatically analyzes the sentiment of your text.

4

Community Review

Once approved by moderators, your feedback appears in the Hall of Fame for everyone to see and vote on.
